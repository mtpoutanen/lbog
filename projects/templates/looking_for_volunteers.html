Have you ever wondered why the internet is full of free, high-quality software. Whilst some of it isn't technically free but instead supported by advertisements, there are thousands upon thousands of software developers who write incredible software for the sheer joy of seeing their creations in use across the world. These are the types of people we target as potential volunteers and aim to give the opportunity to use their skills for charitable projects.

Our potential volunteers include teams of developers, solo developers with years of experience as well as more junior developers eager to help. So whether you need an application to help in fundraising, a simple website, or something else entirely, we might be able to help. In case you have already registered and created a project, you can also look for developers that meet your needs on this page.
